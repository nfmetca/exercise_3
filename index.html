Tribute to Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson

Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson is an American actor, producer, and former professional wrestler who rose to fame through his larger-than-life persona in WWE. Born on May 2, 1972, in Hayward, California, Johnson initially pursued a football career before shifting to wrestling, where he became one of the most iconic figures in the industry. His charisma and talent led to a successful transition into Hollywood, where he starred in blockbuster films like the Fast and Furious series, Jumanji, and Moana. Beyond his entertainment career, Johnson is known for his philanthropy, business ventures, and inspiring millions with his work ethic and positive attitude.
